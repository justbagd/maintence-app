# ApartmentCare — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
- **Tenants**: Apartment residents reporting maintenance issues (broken heater, leaking pipe, etc.). Often stressed when logging in — something is wrong at home and they need to know someone will fix it.
- **Maintenance Staff**: Technicians managing their assigned work queue. Need clarity and efficiency — no fluff.
- **Admins**: Property managers overseeing all requests, staff assignment, and analytics. Need confident data density.

### Brand Personality
**Modern · Efficient · Clean**

The emotional goal is **security and trust** — when a tenant logs in to report a broken heater, the interface should communicate "someone is on this." The product should feel competent and calm, not bureaucratic.

### Aesthetic Direction
A blend of **Airbnb** (warm, human-centered, generous whitespace, trust-building, rounded and approachable) and **Apple** (precision typography, restraint, premium feel, confident layouts, purposeful motion).

- **Light mode only**
- **Warm whites** not pure white — slightly warm off-white backgrounds (#FAFAF8 or similar)
- **Rich navy/blue** as primary — confident, not default corporate
- **Generous whitespace** — Apple-style breathing room; nothing crowded
- **Rounded but not bubbly** — Airbnb-style warmth without being toy-like
- **Small purposeful animations** for the demo — state changes, transitions, entrance reveals

**Anti-references**: Must NOT look like a government form. Must NOT look AI-generated (no cyan-on-dark, no purple gradients, no generic Inter + rounded card combos, no gradient text).

### Typography
- **Display/Headings**: `Plus Jakarta Sans` — modern geometric with friendly curves; sits between Airbnb's Cereal and Apple's SF Pro
- **Body**: `DM Sans` — clean, warm, highly legible
- Load both from Google Fonts

### Design Principles
1. **Trust through clarity** — users always know the status of their request; no ambiguous states, no jargon
2. **Warm precision** — human warmth from Airbnb + technical restraint from Apple; never sterile, never casual
3. **Generous space** — layouts breathe; tight groupings with generous separations between sections
4. **Purposeful motion** — animations signal state changes and guide attention; nothing decorative for its own sake
5. **Premium restraint** — fewer elements, higher quality; every element earns its place
